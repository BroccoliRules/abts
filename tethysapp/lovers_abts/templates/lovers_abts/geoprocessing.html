    IDENTIFYING ZONES FOR FOREST USE

    Geoprocessing Functions Needed

    - One of the more important items of this Landslipe app is the geoprocessing functions used in ArcGIS Pro that allow the app to function and solve problems.
    Our team has used several functions including: Slope, Reclassify, Multi-ring Buffer, and intersect.
    The slope and reclassify will be used with the DEM data of Columbia to highlight the dangerous slope areas.
    The Multi-ring Buffer will be used to show areas prone to landslides around rivers and bodies of water.
    The final outputs of the previous items will be intersected with protected areas to produce the final output of all areas that should have trees planted to resduce landslides.
